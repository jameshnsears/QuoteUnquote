Privacy Policy

The Quote Unquote application is Open-source software: it is provided at no cost, and generates no income.

It is an educational "Fair Use" application.

This page is meant to inform you of the policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal Information if you decide to use the application.

Information Collection and Use

The application is split into two variants:

  • a Google Play variant
  • a F-Droid variant

The Google Play variant uses the following platforms:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase
  • Google Cloud Platform
  • Microsoft Visual Studio App Center

Any analytical information collected by the Google Play variant is anonymous.

The F-Droid variant does not collect analytics.

The application does not use any other service that may collect information about its users.
